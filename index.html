PassGuardian

PassGuardian is an innovative password management solution that keeps your sensitive information close to home. Unlike cloud-based password managers, SecurePass stores your passwords directly in the local storage of the device where the website is running. This unique approach offers a heightened level of security, ensuring that your passwords are always under your control.

Bubble Pop Challenge

Bubble Pop Challenge is an exhilarating web-based game that puts your number-spotting skills to the test! With just 30 seconds on the clock, you'll immerse yourself in a whirlwind of popping bubbles, each hiding a unique number. Your mission is simple: spot and click on the number that matches the target value displayed at the top of your screen. Fast reflexes and a sharp eye are your keys to success in this addictive game.
